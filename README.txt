This is a simple web application built with Django that allows users to perform CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations on a database of drivers and cars.


Create
To create a new driver or car, click on the "Create" button on the home page and fill out the form.

Read
To view the list of drivers or cars, navigate to the "Drivers" or "Cars" page.

To view the details of a specific driver or car, click on its name in the list.

Update
To update the details of a driver or car, navigate to its detail page and click on the "Edit" button. Fill out the form with the updated information and click "Save".

Delete
To delete a driver or car, navigate to its detail page and click on the "Delete" button.
